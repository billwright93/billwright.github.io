Research

My research mainly focuses on investigating the effects of modified gravity on large-scale structure formation. One approach to studying structure formation is the use of N-body simulations. With collborators at ICG Portsmouth I helped implement modified gravity in an approximate simulation method called COLA. This work resulted in a paper, as well as a code developed by Hans Winther (who is now at Oslo) is MG-PICOLA.
